Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CoForms and conversational forms

How does CoForms work?

Simply paste your existing form URL from providers like Typeform or Google Forms, and CoForms automatically converts it into an interactive chat interface. No coding required - it's that simple!

Which form providers are supported?

We support all major form providers including Typeform, Google Forms, Microsoft Forms, JotForm, and SurveyMonkey. More integrations are being added regularly.

Will I lose my existing form data?

No, your existing form and all its data remain unchanged. CoForms creates a conversational layer on top of your form while maintaining all your original data collection and integrations.

Can I customize the conversation flow?

Yes, you have full control over the conversation flow, tone, and styling. Customize the chat interface to match your brand and create the perfect experience for your users.

How secure is CoForms?

Security is our top priority. We use enterprise-grade encryption and comply with GDPR and other privacy regulations. Your data remains secure and private at all times.

What about mobile responsiveness?

CoForms is fully responsive and optimized for all devices. The chat interface is especially suited for mobile users, making form filling easy and engaging on smartphones and tablets.
